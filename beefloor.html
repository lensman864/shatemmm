BEEFLOOR

Beefloor Compact Heterogenious Floor Coverings are inflammable and so does not continue burning unless other flammable materials are extinguished. Chlorine found in suspension PVC comes out when contacting to fire and forms a natural shield against oxygen which keeps burning rate below 100 mm/minute.

There is a 100% pure PVC based top wear layer manufactured according to related international normes in all Beefloor Compact Heterogenionus Floor Coverings.

Beefloor Compact Heterogenious Floor Covering are manufactured 100% recylable.
