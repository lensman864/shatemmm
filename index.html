About Us

SHATEM LIMITED an indigenous Engineering, Construction and interior design firm, was incorporated in 1987 under the Nigeria Company Act of 1968 to render service in the construction industry, with an excellent performance till date.

  • The company which commenced operation in 1987 is fully engaged in:-

    • Construction of building & civil engineering structures.
    • Construction of specialist industrial building systems and steel framed structures.
    • Provision of project management services in the construction industry.
    • Interior design and wood construction.

  • SCOPE AND SERVICES

    • Site layout planning.
    • Structure remedial design and rehabilitation for collapsed building.
    • Renovation and redesign building to client desire.
    • Interior design and woodwork, door/frame, ceiling, wardrobe kitchen cabinet.
    • Construction of estate housing development.
    • Construction of industrial factories and steel or concrete frame.
    • Planning and implementing of total project design.
    • Site layout planning.
    • Construction of residential infrastructure and social amenities as estate roads drainage system central sewage treatment system, sports hall/arena, recreational centers, water supply systems, culverts and retaining wall.
