SHATEM LIMITED

SHATEM LIMITED an indigenous Engineering, Construction and interior design firm, was incorporated in 1987 under the Nigeria Company Act of 1968 to render service in the construction industry, with an excellent performance till date.

The company which commenced operation in 1987 is fully engaged in:-

  • Construction of building & civil engineering structures.
  • Construction of specialist industrial building systems and steel framed structures.
  • Provision of project management services in the construction industry.
  • Interior design and wood construction.
